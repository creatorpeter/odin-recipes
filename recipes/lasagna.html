Lasagna Recipe

Description

ere we have a recipe for overnight oats. This is a very simple meal/snack (whichever your preference). It has on average 35g protein. Enjoy!

Ingredients
  • cheese
  • ground beef
  • red sauce
Recipe
  1. boil 2 qt water
  2. insert pasta and boil for 11 minutes
  3. place pasta to a baking sheet
  4. lay ground beef and cheese on layer 1
  5. repeat steps 3 and 4 2 more times
  6. preheat oven to 400 F
  7. insert baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes
